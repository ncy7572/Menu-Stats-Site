## App Information

Menu Stats is a macOS menu bar utility that shows live system activity for:

- CPU
- GPU on supported Apple Silicon Macs
- Memory
- Swap
- Network throughput

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Why does GPU usage not appear on my Mac?

GPU readings rely on Apple Silicon system counters. On unsupported Macs, GPU usage may be unavailable.

### Does Menu Stats collect or upload data?

No. Menu Stats reads system information locally on your device and does not collect, store, or transmit personal data.
